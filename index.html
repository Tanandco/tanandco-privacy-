Privacy Policy

Tan & Co respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our services, including WhatsApp Business messaging, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok integrations, and wellness center operations.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information (name, phone number, email address)
  • Account information from social media platforms (username, profile ID)
  • Service usage data and appointment history
  • Health and safety information related to our tanning services
  • Payment and transaction information
  • Content analytics for social media publishing

This information is collected solely for the purpose of operating our wellness center, managing customer relationships, and providing our services.

2. How We Use Information

Collected data is used to:

  • Send appointment reminders and service notifications via WhatsApp
  • Process bookings and payments
  • Manage customer memberships and packages
  • Share health and safety information related to our services
  • Automate content publishing and track performance on social media
  • Improve service functionality and customer experience

We do not sell or distribute user data to any third party for marketing purposes.

3. WhatsApp Business Messaging

We use WhatsApp Business API to send important notifications to customers who have opted in to receive messages. These include:

  • Appointment reminders using pre-approved message templates
  • Membership renewal and package expiration notifications
  • Service updates and booking confirmations
  • Health and safety information

All WhatsApp messages are sent using Meta-approved templates and comply with WhatsApp Business messaging policies. Customers can opt out at any time.

4. Social Media Integration

When you connect your social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok) to our platform, we access limited information necessary to publish content and provide analytics. This data is used exclusively for the services you've authorized and is not shared with third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. This includes:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Secure authentication systems
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for providing our services or as required by applicable law. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
